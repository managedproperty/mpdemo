Frequently Asked Questions

Answers to common queries from owners and tenants

General FAQs

How do I switch to Managed Property?

It's simple. Just fill out our "Make the Switch" form or give us a call, and we’ll take care of the rest—even notifying your current manager.

Do you manage properties outside Brisbane?

Our focus is on Brisbane, where we have strong local expertise. For properties outside our core area, we’ll let you know if we can help—or refer you to someone we trust.

Can I access my statements online?

Absolutely. Owners receive secure online access to statements, maintenance history, and important documents 24/7 via our client portal.

What happens if my tenant stops paying rent?

We act quickly, following strict legislative procedures. We also offer landlord insurance recommendations to protect your income in the rare event of default.

How often are inspections conducted?

We conduct routine inspections approximately every 3-4 months and provide detailed reports with photos.
