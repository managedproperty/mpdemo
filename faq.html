Frequently Asked Questions

Helpful info for landlords and tenants

Do you advertise on realestate.com.au and domain.com.au?

Yes. All rental listings are advertised as Premiere Listings on realestate.com.au—the highest-ranking option for maximum exposure. We also list on domain.com.au and other major platforms as Standard Listings.

Through our subscription arrangement, we’re able to pass on significant savings to property owners—keeping your marketing cost low, with a capped fee of $221 incl. GST when a new campaign is required.

Do you thoroughly screen tenants?

Absolutely. We require detailed documentation, complete reference checks and tenancy database searches, and present a clear summary and recommendation for your approval. A strong tenant benefits both you and us.

Do you manage council rates, water bills, and body corporate levies?

Yes. Once authorised, we process these invoices from the rent collected and itemise them clearly in your disbursement statement.

Do you provide end-of-financial-year statements?

Yes, and they’re emailed to you at no additional cost.

Do you handle maintenance issues?

Yes. We coordinate all repairs through a network of qualified, reliable contractors—built over years of experience. We do not accept referral fees, and repairs are paid from the rent collected.

How are your management fees calculated?

We charge a flat weekly fee based on the size and complexity of your property. This allows us to deliver high-quality service without surprises.
