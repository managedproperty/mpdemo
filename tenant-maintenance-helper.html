Lights Not Working

  • 💡 Try replacing the light globe if you have a spare
  • 🧪 Test a known working globe in the fitting
  • 🪛 Check that the light switch isn’t a dimmer that’s turned down
  • ⚡ If multiple lights are out, check your switchboard for any tripped circuits

Appliance Power Issue

  • 🔌 Try plugging the appliance into a different power point
  • 🧪 Try a different appliance in the same socket
  • 🔎 Check your switchboard for any tripped circuit breakers
  • ⚠️ Do not attempt to open or repair any appliances yourself
Emergency Electrical Contact:
SEQ Data & Electrical — 1300 403 050

Toilet Blocked or Not Flushing

  • 🚽 Avoid flushing again if the water level is high
  • 🪠 Try a plunger if available
  • 💧 Ensure no items other than toilet paper were flushed

Leaking or Noisy Tap

  • 💦 Ensure the tap is fully closed
  • 🔧 Try turning the handle firmly but not forcefully
  • 🧼 Look for any visible leaks or pooling water
  • 📞 If water is running continuously, treat as urgent
Emergency Plumbing Contact:

No Hot Water

  • ⚡ Check if the hot water system is electric or gas
  • 🔌 For electric systems, check the switchboard for a tripped breaker
  • 🔥 For gas systems, ensure the pilot light is on
  • 🚿 Is it off-peak? You may need to wait for the next cycle
